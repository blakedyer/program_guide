Description

Catalog description

Principles of seismology, gravity, geomagnetism, heat flow, and how they contribute to our understanding of whole Earth structure and plate tectonics.

Supplemental Notes

Catalog notes

  • Credit will be granted for only one of EOS 427, PHYS 427.
  • Normally offered in alternate years. Students are advised to consult with the department to determine when this course is offered.
