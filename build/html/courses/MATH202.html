Description

Catalog description

Vectors, curves, and surfaces in space; partial differentiation; directional derivatives and the gradient vector; Taylor's theorem for a function of two variables; introduction to differential equations.

Supplemental Notes

Catalog notes

  • Credit will be granted for only one of MATH 202, MATH 200, MATH 205.
  • Not open to students with credit in MATH 201 or MATH 204.
