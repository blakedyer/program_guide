Published Description

Catalog text

This program is offered by the School of Earth and Ocean Sciences in the Faculty of Science. In addition to the requirements below, students must consult their Faculty program requirements.

Program Notes

Catalog notes

Program notes

  • BIOL 490D, EOS 490 or 1.5 units of 400-level MRNE coursework offered through the Bamfield Marine Sciences Centre may be used in lieu of one of BIOL 319...PHYS 441 if an appropriate oceans-related topic is chosen, and with permission of the school.
  • Only one Minor may be declared on any degree program.
