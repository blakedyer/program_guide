Published Description

Catalog text

This single degree program is composed of a selected combination of courses from the School of Earth and Ocean Sciences and the Department of Biology. Students complete this program in the Faculty of Science and must consult the Faculty Program Requirements.

Program Notes

Catalog notes

Program notes

  • Completion of a field school as an EOS elective is recommended. EOS 300 requires that EOS 202 be taken as an elective. EOS 401 requires two of 312, 313 or (314 or 431) be taken as electives.
  • Most 400-level EOS classes are offered in alternate years. A schedule of when each class is offered is available on the School of Earth and Ocean Sciences website.
