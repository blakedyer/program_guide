Published Description

Catalog text

This single degree program is composed of a selected combination of courses from the School of Earth and Ocean Sciences and the Department of Chemistry. Students complete this program in the Faculty of Science and must consult the Faculty Program Requirements.

Program Notes

Catalog notes

  • Students with Chemistry 11 and Pre-Calculus 12 or equivalents must take CHEM 091 before enrolling in CHEM 102. Students with Chemistry 12 and Pre-Calculus 12 or equivalents should not take CHEM 091.
  • EOS 120 is a prerequisite to EOS 313.
  • EOS 325 requires a computing prerequisite, which EOS 314 satisfies. EOS 314 should therefore be taken in the third year, and EOS 325 in fourth year, unless EOS 325 prerequisites are met through other computing experience.
  • Most 400-level EOS classes are offered in alternate years. A schedule of when each class is offered is available on the School of Earth and Ocean Sciences website.
